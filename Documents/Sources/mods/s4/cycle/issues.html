Incompatible Mods

  • MC Woohoo by Deaderpool - This causes the try for baby interactions to not consider fertility. Normal woohoo works as intended, but use of the risky woohoo setting will likely cause this to break too.
  • Wicked Whims by TURBODRIVER - The, uh, "woohoo" interactions in this mod probably won't trigger sperm to transfer between sims.
